Nuestra Historia

una obsesión hermosa

Quiéreme began with a simple belief: that a fragrance should be read like a poem, not a label. Every scent in this collection was built as a small story — a first spray, a wear, a linger — because the way a perfume changes on your skin over the hours is more honest than any single note ever could be.

Fourteen fragrances, four collections, one obsession: that scent should feel like something you fell into, not something you simply put on. The Night Collection is for the version of you that doesn't apologize. The Luxury Floral line is quiet confidence. The Atmospheric & Earthy scents are for the days you want to smell like weather, not perfume. And Cozy & Nostalgic exists for the afternoons you want to disappear into softness.

This brand is still young — still being built, bottle by bottle, word by word. Thank you for being here this early.
